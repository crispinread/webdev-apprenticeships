What We Do

(DIFFERENTLY)

We recruit people, often from disadvantaged backgrounds and underepresented groups,
then we train and coach them through an 18 month work placement
with a web development team in the Leeds City Area.

1. Expert Training

The entire curriculum is devised, created, reviewed and delivered by experienced, practicing web developers. Many of them are experts in their fields.

2. Business Focus

We have a stringent selection process and provide pre-apprenticeship training in essential skills to ensure our candidates can hit the ground running. We also offer free consultancy and advice for mentors and team leads.

3. Social Good

Recruitment is focussed on less privileged and underrepresented groups and profits from the scheme are directed to outreach programmes promoting inclusion and diversity.

How much does it cost?

The selection process, pre-apprenticeship training and an additional 3 months of supported learning at the end of the training are completely free. We only charge for the 15 months of group training.

Non-Levy Paying Companies (most SMEs)

Training is 90% funded by the government so cost to business is just £110 a month for 15 months.

Levy Paying Companies

Costs for the training is £16,500 per candidate recouped directly through the levy system.

Salary

The employer pays the apprentice wages - a typical starting salary is £9-£18k pa (£5-£10 ph) dependant on experience and location.
